Astronomy is the scientific study of celestial bodies (such as stars, planets, comets, nebulae, clusters, galaxies) and phenomena originating outside the universe (such as cosmic background radiation). For those of you who are new to learning, you should start from studying easily observable objects such as the Moon, Saturn, Jupiter, constellations ... then gradually other objects as experienced.
