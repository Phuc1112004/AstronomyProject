KITE YOU CAN KNOW ABOUT GALAXIES !

The galaxy contains many planets, star systems, star clusters, and various types of interstellar clouds. In between these objects is the interstellar medium consisting of gas, dust, and cosmic rays. Supermassive black holes are located at the centers of most galaxies. They may be the source for the active galactic nuclei found at the center in some galaxies. Astronomers also know that at the center of the Milky Way is at least one of these massive black holes
